# **App Name**: Caminhando Com Jesus

## Core Features:

- Daily Devotional Content: Provide a daily Bible verse, reflection, guided prayer, and creative activity for families to engage with in a short 10-minute session.
- Content Adaptation Tool: Generative AI tool to adapt Bible verses and reflections to be age-appropriate and easily understood by children, enhancing their engagement and comprehension. The LLM tool decides when and if simplification or added context is needed.
- Progress Tracking: Allow families to track their progress through the 30-day devotional, marking completed activities and reflecting on their journey.
- Kirvano Integration: Integrate with Kirvano for secure purchase and immediate access to the devotional content upon payment.
- Bonus Content Delivery: Provide immediate access to bonus launch content upon purchase for a limited time to incentivize early adoption.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A warm, inviting yellow (#FFD700) to evoke feelings of joy, hope, and divinity.
- Background color: A light, desaturated yellow (#FAF8F0) to provide a soft, gentle backdrop that enhances readability.
- Accent color: A gentle orange (#FFB347) to highlight interactive elements and call-to-action buttons.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern sans-serif with an elegant and fashionable feel.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, for improved readability in longer blocks of text.
- Use gentle and cheerful icons related to family, faith, and learning to create a friendly and engaging atmosphere.
- Follow a clear, sectioned layout inspired by the provided image to ensure that each piece of information has an easy and intuitive reading order.
- Use subtle animations and transitions to make the app feel more engaging and dynamic, especially when navigating between daily devotional activities.
